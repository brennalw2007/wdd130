About Me

Hi! I'm Brenna, welcome to my WDD 130 homepage. I love programming, the outdoors, art, creative writing, and sharks. I'm autistic, and so you'll find I can spend hours on projects without even knowing where the time went. I'm working on a dystopian trilogy, and one of my friends has told me that it's right in league with The Hunger Games. I'm not sure if it's that good, but I am very proud of how it's coming along. Writing is going to be my side business and I'll be doing Software Dev as my main career. Hence being in this class! I am a tried and true member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, and I do my best to use the gifts God has given me to serve him and please him. I am a compassionate, caring person with a creative soul. I plan to start an instagram page in the near future to share my creative talents. I want to share the music I write and my art, especially the songs and art pieces that have to do with the gospel, in hopes they help someone else as much as they have helped me. Through my work, I want people to feel God's love for them.

Arizona

I live in Snowflake, Arizona. I love it here, and I am a desert girl at heart. The image below is of two kit foxes playing. Kit foxes are native to Arizona and can be found in most places throughout the state. You can also really see the beauty of Arizona in general in this photo. Golden Hour in Arizona is immensely beautiful. We also have many beautiful desert plants, like the famous saguaro cactus, or the fascinating palo verde tree.
