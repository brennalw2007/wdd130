Trips: Raft Away From it All

Public Trips

Public trips are easy to book and a lot of fun. Just reserve the number of people, pick a date and time, and join other groups on a big trip. You can reserve up to eight seats per group, the equivalent of one raft.

Private Trips

Private trips are the perfect trip if you have something specifc in mind. The location? your choice. How many rafters? Your choice. You can book a trip with us to go to any location with any group. We'll provide the equipment and an adventure guide to keep you and your group safe.

Kid-Friendly Trips

Got little ones? No worries. We offer trips on calmer rivers, with extra adventure guides to ensure safety. You and your children are in good hands here, and Kid-Friendly trips are the perfect pick if you have younger kids in your group.

Trip Pros Cons
Public Trips Affordable, less planning Less customizable
Private Trips You choose location, any size group. More Setup on your part, you pay for employee's flight.
Kid-Friendly Trips Bring the whole family, safe for young children Due to extra staff, only six members of your party to a raft.
