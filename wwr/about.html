Wolf Whitewater

At Wolf Whitewater, we are devoted to creating an experience you'll never forget. This is the place every nature-loving thrill seeker feels at home. This is it, the once-in-a-lifetime, long-distance whitewater rafting adventure.

History

Wolf Whitewater was founded in 1999 with one simple goal in mind- create an experience that brings people together. We started with just a handful of rafts and a love for the water. Since then, we've grown into a full blown company with a signature whitewater experience. We hope you'll join us out on the rapids!
